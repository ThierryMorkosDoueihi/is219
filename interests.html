My Interests

What do I like?

Music

Since I was a kid, I had an interest in music. When I was about 8 years old, my parents took me to my first piano lesson. Even though I stopped going after a couple of times, I kept practicing by myself and started playing by ear. Later on I started taking guitar lessons. Three years into that, my friend introduced me to EDM and this is what really caught my attention. A little bit of research, a lot of time listening to music and watching professional DJ performing in live shows was all it took before I realized that I wanted to be a DJ. Started by using the virtual DJ free software and after some improvments, I finally bought my own professional set: the Numark NDX400. A lot of practice made become a better DJ and then I took my first step into the industry.

Soccer

I have always enjoyed all types of sports, but soccer was my favourite. I started playing that game since I was very young. It all started when my neighbor and I bought our first ball and started playing on the street next to the building we lived in. Then I decided to take the next step by joining an academy. At that time, I was already in a basketball academy, but I have decided to quit since it was clear to me that I enjoy soccer a lot more. From that moment it became my obsession. The only places to find me if I am not at practice nor school, would be on the street playing more soccer, or in my bedroom watching soccer videos. Soon I started improving a lot and at that time, I was seen as the team's leader especially when we started competing in tournaments. As the years went by, I kept improving until I became the team's captain and I got drafted to the National Soccer Team, at the age of 14. Unfortunately, about a year and half later I decided to give up on my dream of becoming a professional player and focus on my education. I stepped back a little bit and gave it less time but I kept playing frequently: at least 5 hours a week.
When I got into college, I started practicing again with my old team, but I knew that it was going to be no more than a hobby of mine. A year later, I transferred to William Paterson University and joined there men's soccer team for one year before transeferring again to NJIT.

Painting

The first lesson I attended was when I was 10 years old and went with my friend to his lesson. The artist who was teaching hime gave me a lot of attention and helped draw my first pastel drawing. It was a pear leaning on a vase and it is still hung on the wall in my parents appartment. With a little bit of his help, the outcome turned out to be really good and he suggested that I should start taking lessons. Since that day, I started taking lessons weekly for over 6 years and due to my improvements, I started drawing oil paintings too. Eventually, the lessons became more of a time for me to draw peacfully in a quiet place, and all the teacher did was correct me sometimes and/or give me his personal opinion. Ultimetely, I stopped taking classes but I kept dawing in my free time. Till this day, my parent's appartment is still full of my paintings. You can find them on every wall of every room.

Martial Arts

Until this day, I am a big fan of martial arts. when I was still 6 years old, I went to my first Karate lesson. I trained with a professional Karateka and got my yellow belt in 3 years before switching to Taekwondo. I did not enjoy it as much, thus it did not take me long before quitting. A couple years later, I picked up Muay Thai as my new martial art hobby but unfortunately, the class had a very small amount of people registered in it and they had to cancel it.
